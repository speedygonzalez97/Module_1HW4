Methysko National Park

About the Park

One of the finest and most visited parks in this country, Methysko National Park is home to some of the most rarest rocks and gems ever discovered on our planet! Some such rocks and gems like: Scheelite, Chalcanthite, Painite, Pruskite, and Amethyst, can be found in our park! We have a lot of trees, mountains, lakes, and walking trails for visitors to enjoy and get a feel of our nature. You may ambush some animals such as Stags, Bison, Marmots, Eagles, Elk, Foxes, Owls, Bunnies, and Black bears. We have a large cave called the Luminous Cave, which is home to the Amethyst gems and its a very beautiful cave that you will regret never visiting in your lifetime! Fun fact, Methysko is named after the ancient greek name of the Amethyst gem. Have fun in our park and we hope that your discover, learn, and enjoy the outdoors of nature!

We hope you discover these spots in our park!
